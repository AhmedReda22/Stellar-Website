## About ISPOR International

ISPOR is the leading global organization for Health Economics and Outcomes Research (HEOR), dedicated to advancing healthcare decision-making worldwide. With over 20,000 members across more than 120 countries, ISPOR connects healthcare professionals, researchers, and policymakers to shape policies, improve healthcare outcomes, and promote access to quality care for all. Through its global influence, ISPOR fosters knowledge sharing, innovation, and collaboration that continue to enhance healthcare systems internationally.

## ISPOR UAE Chapter

[ISPOR UAE Chapter](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ispor-uae-chapter/) stands as a cornerstone of healthcare innovation in the United Arab Emirates. This regional chapter of the global organization serves as a platform for professionals in healthcare economics, outcomes research, and related fields to collaborate, learn, and contribute to the advancement of evidence-based healthcare decisions.

The [ISPOR UAE Chapter](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ispor-uae-chapter/) offers a diverse range of activities and initiatives — including educational workshops, conferences, and webinars — while fostering networking among healthcare professionals to facilitate the exchange of ideas and experiences.

## Partnership Overview — ISPOR UAE Chapter × Stellar Consulting MEA

[ISPOR UAE Chapter](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ispor-uae-chapter/) is a leading platform advancing health economics and outcomes research, bringing together experts, policymakers, researchers, and industry leaders. ## A Transformative Scientific Program: Exclusive Senior Health Policy Makers Summit

The first-ever Senior Health Policy Makers Summit — a landmark gathering of national and global health policy leaders

A significant milestone was the launch of the **Exclusive Senior Health Policy Makers Summit**, organized for the first time, bringing together national and global health policy leaders under one umbrella. This high-level gathering strengthened strategic dialogue, encouraged cross-border collaboration, and reinforced the conference's role as a platform influencing healthcare decision-making at the highest levels.

The Summit focused on developing solutions to improve population health outcomes, optimize resource allocation, expand access to innovative therapies, and ensure the long-term sustainability of healthcare systems in the region.
